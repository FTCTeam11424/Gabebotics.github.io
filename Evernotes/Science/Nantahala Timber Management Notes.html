As of 2011, prescribed burns are permitted in the Nantahala forest for:
-Cove hardwood 
-Upland hardwood
-White pine
-Yellow pine
-Northern Hardwood 
-Spruce/fir
Over 20 different management areas with various types of management plans. 
Goals of management plan:
  1. Mix needs of the people and environment.
  2. Improve quality of life for citizens
  3. Maintain/Advance diversity of plants/animals
  4. Rehabilitate areas destroyed by logging/fires.
  5. Take advantage of the forests ability to have large trees
  6. Reduce use of clearcutting and use two-age or selection methods.
  7. Protect aesthetic value
  8. provide env./social settings
  9. Integrate science
  10. Keep public involved

Management plan says: Reduce clear-cutting and use prescribed burns when possible. Since the forest has such valuable timber, use selection method in order to provide for the economy.
