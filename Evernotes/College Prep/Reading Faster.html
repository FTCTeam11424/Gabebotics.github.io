Scan the Tex
  • Scan the text, paying attention to clues suc as bold or italicized text, bullet points, and all headings and subheadings. Pay special attention to the white space as well. 
  • Look first at the first two paragraphs and the last two paragraphs. This will give you a good idea about the overall theme of the article


Margin Notes
  • Margin notes are important for quick recall during the SAT or ACT. If you can, jot down a few notes that summarize the idea of each paragraph.

Chunk
  • Chunking is a technique in which you scan chunks of words, versus reading every word individually
  • You can practice by using your pencil to point to chunks of text (3-5 words) and minimize subvocalization 

Practice
  • Comprehension must be consistent. Your aiming for 75% comprehension on reading tests
  • This means that you can read at a certain rate and recall most important information
