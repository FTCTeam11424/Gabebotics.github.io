In 1961 Stromatolites, which were only previously discovered in fossil formations, were discovered in Shark Bay, on the remote northeast coast of Australia. This was unexpected, in fact, it was some years before scientists realized what they had found. Shark bay is one of the only tourists attractions in about a dozen miles. Boardwalks have been built on the bay that allows visitors to go over the water to view the Stromatolites which quietly respire beneath the surface of the water.
