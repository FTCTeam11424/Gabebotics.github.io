Facts 
  • India is the 7th largest country in the World
  • 2nd largest population on Earth (15% of the World’s people live in India) 
  • A little over 2% of total land mass of World 
  • Himalayas separate it from most of Asia 
  • Arabian Sea on the West, Bay of Bengal to the East, Indian Ocean to the South (Peninsula) 
  • Rivers flow through India, making robust crops 

Geography-Culture Features 
  • Where you live determines what you eat, your religion, etc. 
  • Haridwar and Varanasi are two of the most Buddhist cities, and lay on the bank of Ganges (Many people think that the waters of the Ganges cleanse sins and cure ill) 

Climate 
  • April and May are hottest months
  • Monsoon season during summer is needed for crops 
    • If its late, then crops die 

Agriculture 
  • Largest industry, 2/3 of population farms 
  • Major crops are rice, cotton, spices, sugarcane, tea (Cash crops) 
  • Largest producer of fruit in the world today
    • Mangoes, bananas, papayas

Mining
  • Iron ore, bauxite (used to make Aluminum), copper, limestone, petroleum 
  • Iron ore was common in India
  • Flint was exploited by Indus Valley inhabitants in 3rd Millennium BCE (Starting fires, weapons)
