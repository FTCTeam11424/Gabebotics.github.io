Mauryan Empire (321-185 BC)
-First Empire of Ancient India
-The Empire spread from Bengal in the east to Afghanistan in the west 

Chandragupta Maurya (324-297 BC)
-Unified northern and southern India into a single state
-Chandragupta was able to maintain a strong central administration of the empire.
-Administration of the empire was assisted by the development of a progressional civil service
Civil Service: the permanent professional branches of a government's administration, excluding the military.

Chanakya (350-275 BC)
-Chief advisor to Emperor Chandragupta 
-He assisted Chandragupta in expansion of the empire and subsequent development of governance
-Chanakya's thoughts on governing were laid out in the political treatise Arthashastra 

Arthashastra
Chankya's political treatise covering governancee, economics, diplomacy, military strategy, and espionage. Was discovered in 1904 - printed on palm wood.    

Diplomacy: The profession, activity, or skill of managing international relations.
Economics: the branch of knowledge concerned with the production, consumption, and transfer, of wealth.
