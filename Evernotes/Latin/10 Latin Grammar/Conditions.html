Si versipellem video, timeo
Present logical
If i see a werewolf, i am afraid

Si versipellem videbam timebam 
Past logical 
If i saw a werewolf, i was afraid. 

Si versipellem video, timebo 
Future more vivid
If i will see a werewolf, i will be afraid

Si versipellem vidissem, timuissem 
Past contrafactual (pluperfect subjunctives) 
Yesterday, If i had seen a werewolf, i would have been afraid

Si versipellem viderem, timerem 
Present contrafactual (Imperfect subjunctive) 
Today, If i were seeing a werewolf, i would be afraidn  

Si versipellem videam, timeas: 
Present subjunctives makes a future less vivid(should/would condition)
Tomorrow If i should see a werewolf, i would be afraid. 
Tomorrow If i were to see a werewolf, i would be afraid.
