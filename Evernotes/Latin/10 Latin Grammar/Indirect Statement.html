Requires:
  1. Head verb: thinking, feeling, saying, knowing, hearing, smelling, sensing, seeing.  (Almost anything that can be followed by that)
  2. Accusative Subject 
  3. Infinitive Verb 
Present head verb: 

(Present-Present) Video tē amare tortum 

I see that you love pie 

Head verb: Video 
Accusative Subject: tē 
Infinitive Verb: amare

(Present- Perfect) Video tē amarisse tortum: I see that you loved pie

(Present-Future) Video tē amaturum esse tortum: I see that you will love pie

(Present-Present Passive) Video tortum amari ā tē: I see that pie is loved by you 

(Present-Perfect Passive) Video tortum amatum esse ā tē: I see that pie was loved by you 

(Perfect-present) Vidi tē amare tortum: I saw that you would love pie

(Perfect-perfect) Vidi tē amarisse tortum: I saw that you had loved pie
