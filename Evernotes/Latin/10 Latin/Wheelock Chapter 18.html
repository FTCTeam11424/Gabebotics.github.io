Hello, o gods and goddesses! Our friend Peleus has a great plan; He is leading the nymph Thetis into marriage. You all therefore have been called by me, Jupiter, to Thessalia. Expect good games and and sweet feasts. However You ought to be warned about these dangers:
  1. Since Peleus is mortal, his spirit would be terrified if we were seen with too much power 
  2. Who dares to go without a gift, will be punished by me.
Gather all of gifts which will be praised by everyone who will see them.

You alone, o goddess Discordia, you have not been called for you love nothing. If you come, every god in Olympus will be moved by anger. Discord between friends will not be tolerated.
Penalties will be paid by the god who neglects their duties. Goodbye!
