Subjunctive Passive

Imperfect: paro, parare, paravi, paratus
  1. count to two
  2. Add passive ending
Pararer Pararemus
Parareris Paararemini
Pararetur Pararentur

Pluperfect: paro, parare, paravi, paratus
  1. count to 4
  2. If plural, change -us to -i.
  3. Add helping verb esse after
  4. Add the active endings to esse
 Paratus essem        Paratus essemus
Paratus  esses.        Paratus essetis
Paratus esset.          Paratus essent
