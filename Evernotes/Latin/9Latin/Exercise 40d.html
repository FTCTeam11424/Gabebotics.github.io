1.Aeneas while looking for Italy arrived in Carthage.
2.Marcus found his father writing letters in his study.
3.I ask the bystanders where the fire was.
4.Eucleides was stricken while walking home at night. 
5.The boys annoyed Davus while he was working in the garden.
6.The girls responded nothing to me.
7.Many of those swimming saw small ships approaching the shores.
8.The voice of the teacher scolding the boys is heard.
9.Cornelius while expecting Eucleides in the atrium heard slaves speaking in the kitchen.
10.Titus met Cornelius, his brother, coming out of the house.
11.Their shouts of rejoice were heard in the street.
12.Cornelius gave the signal for carrying dishes to the slaves in the dining room.
13.The Cornelius' were fearing death in the inn while spending the night 
14.We caught sight of women going to the temple.
15.The girls, who were talking among themselves, were telling many wonderful stories about the boys.
16.We see mother sitting in the atrium,while we are leaving the house.
17.I ordered Sextus to come down while climbing the tree.
