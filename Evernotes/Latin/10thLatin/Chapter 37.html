I was going by the sacred road, as I am accustomed to, thinking about my matters. Some random man known to me only by his name ran, and with my hand having been snatched, said, “How are you doing?” 

“Sweetly ,” I said. When that guy was following, I, wanting to go away miserably, at one time I was going quickly, at another I was stopping. That guy was speaking, praising the roads and Rome. As I was never responding to that guy, he said “You want to go away miserably.”

“I go to the houses of my certain friends, a long way across the Tiber,” I said.

“I have nothing which I am doing, and I am not lazy; I will follow you.” We were coming to the temple Vesta, Now it is after the fourth hour of the day, and he had to respond to his lawsuit; unless he should do this, he would lose his lawsuit. 

    He said, “If you love me stay here as my support!” 

    I said, “I should die, if I know legal law” 

    He said, “What should I do? Should I remain or lawsuit?” 

    “I.”
    “Should do nothing”

    Then Aristides Fuscus, my caring friend, ran up. I snatched his hands, wavering, eyes twisting this way and that, as he rescued me. 
    “Certainly there is something you want to speak with me,” I said 
    “I remember well, but I am speaking of a bad occasion;Forgive me; it is necessary for me to go.” Then he ran and he abandoned me under a knife. 

Then he ran to that adversary: “Why do you go away most disgracefully?” Shouted a loud voice, and “Is it allowed for you to come as a witness?” I gave my ears. He snatched that guy into court. Thus Apollo preserve me.
