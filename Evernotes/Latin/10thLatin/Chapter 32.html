O many fortunate farmers, to whom the richest earth willingly pours food easily. Pleasant leisure, the fields open long and wide, the caves and living lakes, the mooing of the cows, and sweet naps under trees from this they do not leave. Between them, rights and laws, stay for a long time; evils are prohibited. He lives happily who was able to understand causes of matters by reason and by the light of knowledge he expels all fear and the worst anxiety from him. They are neither terrified of that harsh war, nor savage army, nor the rest of the dangers which men often fear. That guy does not love riches and honors so strongly that he wants to lose the benefits of the rustic life. Although they are few, nevertheless they seem to themselves equal to kings, when their small sons quickly ran to them and offered deer kisses most free. The gods having been worshipped, they give perpetual peace to this man. Romulus and Remus once led similar happy lives.
