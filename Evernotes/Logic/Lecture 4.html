What a definition does: 
  1. Tells us what a thing is. 
  1. Best definitions are essential definitions. 
  2. Goal of a definition is to allow the mind to clearly perceive the defined term by itself 
  3. de-finio- to set limits around a thing



8 Kinds of Definitions: 
  1. Nominal Definition - Definition of a word rather than the thing the word refers to.
    1. Ex. This is not a pipe (Picture of a pipe) 
    2. Ex. IPad - A pad with a i in front. (Explains the word, but not the thing itself.) 
    3. Least preferable kind of definition because it is not defining the thing itself. 
  1. Essential Definition - Gives the essence (meaning) of the term in two parts: 
    1. The generic/general common part (genus) 
      1. Genus should be the narrowest Genus as possible. 
    2. Difference from other in genus (Specific Difference) 
    3. Trying to convey the inner meaning of the term. 
    4. This is the most preferable kind of definition. 
    5. Ex. Man is a rational animal (Rational is specific difference, Animal is genus) 
  2. By Properties - Combining all the properties which belong to the essence of a thing
    1. Conveys properties of a thing. 
    2. Ex. “Man is a breathing, sleeping, eating, and thinking creature.” 
  3. By Accidents - Combining all the changeable features of a thing. (All of the accidents) 
    1. Weak way to define a term
    2. Ex. “My Car is a Toyota, Grey, 2003 model” 
  4. By efficient cause (agent) - Defining a thing by what causes it to be produced. 
    1. Uses the origins of a thing. 
    2. Ex. “Son of Brad” - Finding origin
    3. Ex. “An acorn is a seed produced by an oak tree” - finds origin of acorn
  5. By final cause (purpose) - defining a thing by explaining what it is for, the purpose of something. 
    1. Ex. “A house is a machine for living”
    2. Ex. “My car is a machine that transports me to work” 
  6. By material cause (contents) - defining a thing by what it is made of (matter) 
    1. Ex. “Fish are animals with gills” 
    2. Ex. “My car is an animal with a engine, 4 wheels, seat, etc.”
  7. By effects - Defining a thing by what it does to someone or something else
    1. A carcinogen is any thing or event that tends to cause cancer.
