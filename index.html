Gabe's Latin Notes

Result Clause

A main clause with “so”, or “in such a way”
Has a word that mean that

When you see these words:
Adeo, Ita, Sic, Talis, -is, -e, Tam, Tantus, -a, -um, Tantum, Tot
There will often be ut later in the sentence, which shows that it is a result clause.

Example: Your Momma jokes Your Mom is so ____ that ______
Mother is SO busy THAT she never speaks to me.
Mater tam occupata est ut numquam mecum loquatur
Father spends so much time in his study that I never see


Indirect Question

Starts with head verb
Has a question word such as cur.
Has a subjunctive verb

Example:
Scio cur Marcus ad amphitheatrum iret
I know why Marcus is going to the ampitheater
